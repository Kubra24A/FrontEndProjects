Tuscany

Tuscany, known as the place where the sun sets most beautifully, is the pupil of the city of Florence. Tuscany, one of the most tourist-attracting places in Italy, is known for its cultural richness and calm lifestyle from past to present. The homeland of lavender and vineyards, Tuscany was the most powerful city in Florence, one of the most important cities in the world in the 13th century. With its endless green valleys, vineyards, medieval castles and towns, it has been placed on the World Heritage List by UNESCO.
