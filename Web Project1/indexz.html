LASAGNA

HİSTORY

Lasagna the name and the dish is of ancient origin. There are various theories about the word, such as the Latin lasanum for “cooking pot,” or the ancient Greek and Roman laganum for “flat piece of bread”. Within Italy, still today, variations of the name persist, such as sagne or lagana. Lasagna is a delicious flavor of Italian cuisine, made with pasta dough, packaged in plates, often baked in the oven with bolognese sauce.

INGREDİENTS:
  • 1 packet of lasagna leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 500 grams of medium-fat minced meat
  • 2 carrots (diced)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cups grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 grated tomatoes
  • 2 crushed garlic
  • 2 teaspoons salt and pepper
    • FOR BECHAMOL SAUCE :
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 4 cups cold milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon grated muscat walnuts
CONSTRUCTION :

Ground beef is roasted with oil, onion, garlic. Tomato paste, canned tomatoes, water and spices are added and cooked over heavy heat for 10-15 minutes. By the way, lasagna is boiled and filtered according to the recipe in the box. In a separate bowl, mix the mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese and egg well.

he oven is heated to 180 degrees. Borcam is put in the baking dish with a scoop of minced meat sauce ( this prevents the lasagna dough from sticking to the tray). A layer of lasagna dough is lined up over the sauce and minced meat sauce is spread over it. The second layer of lasagna dough is lined up and the curd cheese we have prepared is rubbed into the mixture and the mozzarella cheese is put. The minced meat sauce spreads again. The same process is applied sequentially until all the ingredients are finished. Mozzarella cheese is spread on top and half a glass of water is added to it. The tray is closed with foil and baked in a preheated oven for 30 minutes.Then the foil is removed and the lasagna is cooked with the mouth open for another 30 minutes. After removing it from the oven, it is let rest for 10 minutes and sliced and served.
